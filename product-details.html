Red Double Sided Tape

Red-sided tape, commonly known as double-sided tape with a red liner, is a versatile adhesive product. It is typically used for mounting, bonding, and securing objects in various industries like crafts, automotive, and home improvement. The red liner is simply a protective covering for the adhesive beneath and is removed during application. This tape can bond well to surfaces such as metal, plastic, wood, and glass, providing a strong and durable hold while being easy to use and apply in a variety of settings.
